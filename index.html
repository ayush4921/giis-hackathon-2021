Frequently Asked Questions
Is the event physical or virtual?
Virtual! We believe that a virtual event will allow for more intuivite activities and help teammates connect better with each other.
Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to have experience in coding?
Depends on what you're looking to make! A little background in programming will be helpful, but you'll have plenty of opportunity to join teams and learn from GIIS students, students from other schools, and mentors.
Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate?
Any student from grades 6-12 (or ages 11-17) can participate in the GIIS Hackathon.
Frequently Asked Questions
How many people can be in a team?
You may join as an individual or with a team of not more than 4 people in total.

Yes, only $10.
